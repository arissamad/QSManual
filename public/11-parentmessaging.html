Parent Messaging

The Parent Messaging is a powerful communications feature that enables schools to send messages to groups of parents or the entire school at one time. Create email and voice message templates for frequently sent messages including school closures, field trip information, or upcoming tuition bills. You can also create and save call lists for groups that are contacted frequently such as the PTA, specific grade levels, or the entire school.

This chapter provides detailed information on creating emails, text messages, and voice messages for mass mailing.

Introduction to Parent Messaging

Parent Messaging is a powerful, highly customizable tool for communicating with groups of parents and students quickly. Using information from the student database, student and parent names and phone numbers are available with the click of the mouse. Create and save call lists for specific groups of people, like the PTA, the debate team, or grade-specific students. Save templates for your most frequently used communications so you do not have to repeatedly recreate the same messages.

Enabling/Disabling Parent Messaging

By default the Parent Messaging feature comes enabled. If your school does not need to use the Parent Messaging feature, you may turn the feature off. The Parent Messaging option is removed from the top menu bar giving you a customized look and feel. Likewise, if the Parent Messaging option is not visible in the top menu bar, it is easily enabled for immediate use within your school.
