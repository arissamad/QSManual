Transcripts

The Transcripts tab provides school administrators with a versatile and fully-customizable transcript system, enabling them to configure and display transcript records for students in a variety of grade levels, ranging from Kindergarten to college-bound high school students. Grade point average (GPA) can be calculated using a customizable GPA table, establishing a centralized formula that can be used school-wide. Transcripts may be configured to display a wide range of information, including student biographical and contact data, attendance, subjects taken, grades and credits earned, GPA (both weighted and cumulative), class rank, SAT/ACT scores, and other customizable fields.

Activating and Configuring Transcripts Feature

Before getting started with the Transcripts tab, it is necessary to make sure that the Transcripts feature is configured for your QuickSchools account, and to confirm who in your school will have the administrative privileges necessary to access, configure, download, and print student transcripts. You will need to first manage your plan through the Features link to make sure that the Transcripts feature is turned on and that the correct users have the access they need to work with student transcripts.

First, find the Features link in the top header of your QuickSchools screen and click on it. This will bring up your school's plan and account page with available features. You must be enrolled in either the Apollo or Athena plan to have access to the Transcripts feature.

Make sure that there is a check in the Transcripts row under your plan. This toggles the feature on so you can work on Transcripts. Then click on the Configure link next to the box to set up Transcripts privileges for your school:

This will bring up a Transcripts popup box in which privileges can be set for personnel in your school. You can designate who in your school may configure, create, and print transcripts. To do so, click in the relevant box and select individual teachers from the drop-down menu, or select All Teachers if you want everyone to be able to access the privilege:

After you have added the personnel you want to have access to these tools, scroll to the bottom of the popup box and click on the red Save button () to register your changes in the system. Now you are ready to access and edit transcripts!

Accessing Transcripts Tab

To access the Transcript section of QuickSchools from your login page, click on the Transcripts link in the gray bar at the top of the screen, as shown:

This will bring up the main Transcripts page, on which you will see up to three different menu tabs:

  • Transcripts
  • Transcripts Configuration
  • Template Selection

The Transcripts tab contains a list of all students in the school. From this page, you can locate, view, download, and print individual student transcripts.

Modifying Display of Student Names on Transcripts Page

The list of students is set by default to display alphabetically; this may be modified so that they appear in reverse alphabetical order, and the number of rows of names per page may be changed to display more (or fewer) than the default value of 10. Alternately, you may use the search feature to display a single student name, or a subset of students with the same surname.

Using the Search Feature

The search feature may be used to select a student record, or subset of student records for which you would like to generate transcripts. You may search by entering the surname, or any part of the student's name in the search box.

Displaying Students in Reverse Alphabetical Order

It is easy to display student records in reverse alphabetical order; click once on the blue border labeled Student next to the white triangle and it will reverse to list them from "Z" to "A."

Modifying Page Display to Change Number of Rows Per Page

The number of records displayed on the screen may be set and/or customized by clicking on this icon () in the very last row in the table. This reveals the following selection menu:

As indicated, the default setting is to display 10 rows on the screen; additional rows will then be shown on subsequent screens. You may change this to enable viewing of 25, 50, or 100 column categories at a time. The number of rows can also be customized to select all records by selecting the Custom selection, in which case you will be prompted to fill in the desired number of rows to be made visible per page by the following popup box:

Enter the desired number of rows to display and click on the Ok button () to save your preference.

Finally, to restore the selection to the default, select Clear Preferences from the selection menu. This will erase any prior selection you may have made and revert to the default view of the list of records on the Transcripts tab.

Selecting Transcripts

Clicking on a student name on the main Transcripts tab will bring up the transcript for that individual, which may then be modified or customized to display different semesters, downloaded, and printed.
