Online Payments

Online payments are easy way for your school to manage and collect online credit card payments. QuickSchools uses the online payment service Stripe, so all you need to do is set up an account. Stripe takes care of processing and recording credit card payments, then sends the records right to your QuickSchools account. Once your Stripe account is set up, parents can easily and conveniently pay their bills with their credit cards online right from their QuickSchools Parent Portal.

This chapter offers detailed information on setting up and managing online payments and setting up and using a Stripe account.

    • Introduction to Online Payments

    Online payments provides a quick and convenient way for parents to submit a credit card payment. Online payments are managed through the Parent Payments page which is accessed through the Student Billing feature. Parents can make credit card payments to your school and view their transactions through their Parent Portal.

    Online payments are collected through Stripe, an online payment service. Once your Stripe account is set up, online payments are easy for your school to collect and manage and simple for parents to setup and use.

    Enabling and Disabling Online Payments

    By default the Online Payments feature comes disabled. After enabling Online Payments, the Parent Payments menu bar option becomes available on the Student Billing page.

    1. From the top of the page, click Features.

      The Features page opens.

    1. Go to the row that says School Fees Tracking.

    1. Click Configure. The School Fees Tracking dialog box opens.

    1. Check Turn on Online Payments in the Parent Portal.

    1. Click the Select Users who will be able to Manage & View Payments by Parents text box. A list of faculty members opens.
    2. From the faculty member list, select who can view and manage online payments.

      3. Note: If you leave this box blank, nobody will be able to view or manage online payments.

    3. Click Save to save your changes. Click Cancel to leave your preferences unchanged.

    Moving around the Parent Payments Table

    By default, 10 categories are displayed on each page of the Parent Payments table. When more than 10 transactions are added, a new page in the Parent Payments table is added. Use the navigation controls at the bottom of the Parent Payments table to scroll through the pages. To change the number of categories shown on each page, see Setting Parent Payments Table Preferences.

    Using Online Payments

    Online Payments are made through Stripe so there is no need for your school to handle or process credit card payments. Online payments are posted to the Parent Payments page and to student’s individual accounts when parents make their payments from the QuickSchools Parent Portal.

    Accessing an Online Payment Record

    To access a record of online payments that parents have made, go to the Parent Payments page.

    1. Click Fee Tracking at the top of the page. The Student Billing page opens.

    1. On the right of the Student Billing page, click Parent Payments. The Parent Payments page opens.

      2. Note: If there is no Parent Payments option at the top of the page, see Enabling and Disabling Online Payments.

    Introduction to Stripe

    QuickSchools uses the online payment service Stripe to manage all online payments to QuickSchools. To receive online payments, you need to setup a Stripe account. A link to Stripe is available within QuickSchools on the Parent Payments page. Once the school's Stripe account is setup, a View and Pay Fees link will be available in the parent portal menu. When parents click View and Pay Fees, they are prompted to enter their credit card information. Once their credit card information has been entered and saved, parents can quickly pay their fees. Generally, online payments are posted to your school bank account by Stripe 7 days after the payment is made.

    QuickSchools charges 4.9% + 30 cents on the total amount paid. For a limited time, we're offering a 3.9% + 30 cents transaction fee for schools who set up Stripe accounts early.

    Setting up a Stripe Account

    1. From the Student Billing table, click Parent Payments. The Parent Payments page opens.
    2. Click Connect with Stripe.

      You are now at the Stripe Website. The Stripe Account Setup page opens.

    1. Complete the Stripe Account Setup form with your school information.
    2. Click Authorize Access to this Stripe Account when you are finished. Your account is setup and you will return to QuickSchools. You may need to log back in to your QuickSchools account.
    3. To access, use, or manage your Stripe account, visit Stripe’s Website at Stripe.com.

    Synchronizing Stripe Payment Records with QuickSchools

    Stripe automatically updates your QuickSchools account when online payments are made from the Parent Portal or refunds are issued by your school. These updates usually occur quickly and require no action on your part. However, if a payment or refund is made through the Parent Portal, but your records are not updated immediately, you can synchronize your records with Stripe. This updates your QuickSchools records with the latest payment and refund information that Stripe has recorded.

    You can synchronize your QuickSchools account 2 ways:

    • Click Synchronize with Stripe from the Parent Payments page.

    • Click Sync with Stripe from the Online Payment Details dialog box.

    Deauthorizing a Stripe Account

    If you no longer want to receive online payments from parents through Stripe, you can deauthorize Stripe from accepting payments on your school’s behalf. Deauthorizing Stripe from your QuickSchools account does not close your Stripe account. To close your Stripe account, go to Stripe.com.

    1. From the Parent Payments page, click Disconnect Stripe Account. A confirmation dialog box opens.

    1. Click Yes to deauthorize Stripe from receiving payments from parents on your school’s behalf or Cancel to leave your Stripe account unchanged.

    Viewing an Online Payment

    All online payments made from the Parent Portal page using Stripe are automatically recorded in your QuickSchools account. To view general online payment information, go to the student’s account. To view detailed online payment information, go to the Parent Payments page.

    Viewing General Online Payment Information

    1. From the Student Billing page, click on the Primary Payer or a student name whose account you want to view. The family account or student account opens.
    2. Click OK when you are finished viewing the account information.

    Viewing Detailed Online Payment Information

    1. From the Parent Payments page, click on the transaction you want to view. The Online Payment Details dialog box opens.

    1. Click OK when you are done viewing the information.

    Sorting Parent Payments

    To find information faster, you can sort table columns by ascending or descending order.

    1. Click on the column that you want to sort. The table sorts information by the selected column in ascending order.
    2. Click on the column again to sort by descending order.

    An arrowhead displays in the column that the table is being sorted by. An arrowhead pointing up indicates the table is sorted in ascending order. An arrowhead pointing down indicates the table is sorted in descending order.

    In this example, the table is sorted by Standard Charge name in ascending order.

    1. Click OK to set the number of rows or Cancel to leave the number of rows unchanged.

    Searching for a Parent Payment

    The search feature enables you to search by date, primary payer, credit card, amount, status or any combination of these parameters.

    1. Place your cursor in the Search field.

    1. Type in the word or value for which you are searching.
    2. You can begin your search two ways:
      • Press Enter on your keyboard.
      • Click the magnifying glass icon at the end of the search field.

      The results of your search display in the Standard Charges table.

    1. To return to the main Standard Charges table, click Clear Search below the Search field.

    Setting Parent Payments Table Preferences

    You can set how many rows you want the Parent Payments table to display on each page. By default, 10 rows are displayed on each page.

    1. Click the preferences icon at the bottom left of the Parent Payments table. A pop-up menu opens.

    1. Select the number of rows you want to display on each page from the pop-up menu. The table automatically updates.
    2. To set a number of rows that is different than the preset options, select Custom. The Number of Rows to Display dialog box opens.
    3. In the text field, type the number of rows you want to display. The number must be between 1 and 1000.
    4. Click OK to set the number of rows or Cancel to leave the number of rows unchanged.
