Student Portal

Students are able to have their own access to their school information. In order for a student to use the portal, they must be given access.
  • Activating the Student Portal Feature
  • Inviting Students

Activating the Student Portal Feature

The student portal must be turned on by the administrator of the school. You activate the student portal by clicking on the Feautres link in the upper menu.

To turn on the feature, place a check mark in the box on the student portal line. A new menu item will appear in the upper menu called Student Portal.

Next, click on the configure link next to the checkbox to customize your student portal. In this configuration window, you can decide what users can manage student invites.

To add users who can invite students, click inside the invitation box. A list of names will appear. Click on the name you want to add and it will be placed in the box. To remove a name, simply click on the X next to the name.

When all changes have been made, click on the "Save" button. You can also cancel any changes simply by clicking on the cancel button.

Inviting Students

To protect privacy, each student must be invited individually to participate so that each student may be given a unique login and password in order to access their information. The system is designed so the administrator is able to setup the email inivitation once and automatically generate a customized email for each student. The student will then click on a link in their email to gain access to the portal.

How to invite students

To invite students, click on the Student Portal menu item. A list of students are displayed with the student in the first column and their invitation status listed in the second column. Note: You can sort your student data by clicking on the invited/not invited/all students links or you can sort by last name by clicking on the Student title at the top of the list. You can also search for a specific student in the search field by typing their last name and clicking search.

If there is no email address listed for the student, click on the Add email link next to their name. Simply put in the correct information and click on ok. Once an email address has been entered, a checkbox will appear as well as the red invite button.

You can invite students individually or select many at time. If they have already been invited their invite button changes to "reinvite". If for some reason you need to reinvite them, click on the reinvite button and an email will automatically be sent to them.

To invite multiple students at one time, select the checkbox next to their name. Once you have selected all the students, click on the Invite Selected Emails button at the bottom of the list. Hint: If you have a lot of students to invite, click on the Select/Unselect All button at the top of the list. All students will be selected. If you need to remove some students from the list just click on the checkbox next to their name and it will remove the checkmark.

Customizing the invitation

To customize the invitation, click on Want to customize the invitation email? Click here. link.

The default email template appears with 3 sections.

  1. Section 1 is the beginning of the email and is editable text. You can change any of this information to customize your message.
  2. Section 2 consists of system generated login information you cannot change.
  3. The 3rd section is the salutation of the email which is editable text.

Once you have made your changes, hit Save.

How to un-invite students

If for some reason you need to remove the login priveledges for a student, click on the Revoke login link next to the students email. A pop-up box will appear asking you to verify your request.
