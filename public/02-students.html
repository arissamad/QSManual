Students

The Students portal is a gateway to a comprehensive database of student-related information, linking basic student directory record information to related data of interest, such as Parents/Siblings, Gradebook, Homework, Attendance, Bills, Medical, Reports and Certificates. QuickSchools also offers administrators the ability to link additional custom fields of their choice to this area of the site.

For teachers, the Students portal is a convenient way to see at a glance which students are enrolled in their classes, as well as follow their progress through the Attendance, Gradebook, and Homework tabs. Parents and students may also find the Student portal extremely useful in keeping track of attendance, homework, and academic development in multiple subjects.
